Accra Chamber of Commerce – Site Plan

Site Name

Accra Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the official hub for businesses in Accra, Ghana. It was selected to emphasize credibility, visibility, and community engagement. Optional domain: accracommerce.org

Site Purpose

The site provides a central hub for businesses in Accra to access membership information, apply online, view upcoming events, and connect with other organizations. It will also highlight chamber initiatives, training opportunities, and advocacy efforts.

Scenarios

  • How do I apply for membership in the Accra Chamber of Commerce?
  • What benefits do different membership levels provide?
  • Where can I find information about upcoming chamber events?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #004080 (used for headings, header background, and footer)

Accent Color: #F39C12 (used for buttons, highlights, and links)

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto Slab – used for titles and headings.

Body Font: Open Sans – used for paragraphs and general text.
